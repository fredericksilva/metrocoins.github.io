Czarparty is a suite of financial tools within a protocol built on top of the Czarcoin Blockchain.

Production

We set out to create the most capable decentralized exchange system ever forged. Only the Original Stakeholders were allowed to participate in the Initial Burn Cycle. Shortly after the Genesis of Czarcoin, 111000111 CZR were forever removed from circulation to create 1,311,057,736,132 XZR.

Security

XZR is special share unit available to every person on the planet. The base system never exposes private keys to a third party without election from its possessor. Pure fundamentals provide the elements of the platform with the potential needed for expansion.

Sustainability

Interaction with common hardware achieves the realization of an environmentally friendly platform. Plans for inflation and various components of economics are set to track that of global growth.

Speed

Real world applications naturally demand realtime drivers of events. Czarparty is no exception and delivers on its promise of instant transactions. Most network confirmations occur within 128 seconds and trades in the billions of units happen flawlessly.
